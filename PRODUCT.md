# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
French business owners, PMEs, entrepreneurs, and local professionals (especially in Lyon and Villeurbanne) seeking custom high-performance web development, site redesigns, or SEO optimization to grow their digital presence.

## Product Purpose
A premium showcase website and portfolio for Sidikoff Digital agency. It communicates professional authority, technical excellence (Next.js/React, Lighthouse 90+), and brings in high-quality business leads.

## Brand Personality
- Technical authority
- Performance-first
- High-end minimalist execution

## Anti-references
- Low-contrast, hard-to-read "elegant grays"
- Over-rounded card containers and bubbly inputs
- AI purple/lila gradient glows and generic stock layouts

## Design Principles
- French local optimization (expert confidence, clear communication)
- Extreme speed and performance visibility (Lighthouse scores, high core vitals)
- Technical refinement over decorative slop

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG 2.2 AA compliant contrast
- Strict prefers-reduced-motion fallback compatibility
- Semantic HTML and keyboard-first accessibility
